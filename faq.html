Frequently Asked Questions

What is the public transport management system?
A system designed to manage and optimize public transportation services.
How do I create an account?
Click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the registration process.
What if I forgot my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link and follow the instructions.

Technical Questions

How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions. You will receive an email with a reset link.
What if I encounter a technical issue?
If you encounter any technical issues, please contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page. Provide as much detail as possible to help us resolve the issue quickly.
Which browsers are supported?
Our system supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using one of these browsers.

Account and Billing

How do I update my billing information?
You can update your billing information by going to the "Account Settings" page and selecting "Billing Information." Make sure to save your changes.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from the "Account Settings" page. Click on "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts. Note that cancellation terms apply as per our policy.
How do I view my payment history?
To view your payment history, navigate to the "Billing" section under "Account Settings." Here, you will find a detailed list of all your transactions.
